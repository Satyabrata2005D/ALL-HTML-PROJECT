Neymar Jr. - Full Biography

-----------------------------------------------------------



Full Name : - Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior

Date of Birth : - February 5, 1992

Place of Birth : - Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, Brazil

Nationality : - Brazilian

Height : - 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in)

Position : - Left Winger / Attacking Midfielder

Introduction

--------------------------------------------------

Early Life (1992 - 2002) →

---------------------------------------------------------------

  • Born into a modest family ; showed football talent from a very young age .
  • His father , Neymar Santos Sr. was a former footballer and became his mentor and manager .
  • Grew up in São Vicente and later moved to Santos , Brazil .
  • Balanced futsal (indoor football) and street football as a child , developing agility , technique and creativity .

Youth Career (2003 - 2009) →

----------------------------------------------------------------------

  • 2003 (age 11) : - Joined Santos FC's youth academy — the same club where legends like Pelé started .
  • Quickly became the star of Santos' youth teams .
  • At 14 , travelled to Spain for trials with Real Madrid and impressed , but chose to remain at Santos FC .

Professional Career

------------------------------------------------------------------

Santos FC ( 2009 - 2013 ) →

----------------------------------------------------------

  • Professional debut in March 2009 at age 17 .
  • Scored 14 goals in his first season ; quickly became one of Brazil's most exciting talents .
  • Helped Santos win multiple titles : -
    • Copa do Brasil (2010)
    • Copa Libertadores (2011) — Santos first since Pelé's era
  • Won South American Footballer of the Year in 2011 and 2012 .
  • Known for dribbling , flair and spectacular goals .

FC Barcelona ( 2013 - 2017 ) →

--------------------------------------------------------------

  • Transferred in June 2013 for about €57 million .
  • Formed the famous attacking trio with Lionel Messi and Luis Suárez .
  • Major achievements : -
    • La Liga = ( 2014 - 2015 ) , ( 2015 - 2016 )
    • UEFA Champions League = ( 2014 - 2015 )
    • FIFA Club World Cup = 2015
    • Copa del Rey = ( 2014 - 2015 ) , ( 2015 - 2016 ) , ( 2016 - 2017 )
  • ( 2014 - 2015 ) season : - scored 39 goals in all competitions and helped Barcelona win the treble .
  • Finished third in the 2015 Ballon d'Or behind Messi and Ronaldo [ because Pessi ( Messi ) is ballon d'or robber ] .

Paris Saint-Germain ( 2017 - 2023 ) →

--------------------------------------------------------------------

  • August 2017 : - Moved to PSG for a world-record fee of €222 million — became the most expensive player in history .
  • Successes with PSG : -
    • Ligue 1 titles = ( 2017 - 2018 ) , ( 2018 - 2019 ) , ( 2019 - 2020 ) , ( 2021 - 2022 ) , ( 2022 - 2023 )
    • Multiple French Cups and Super Cups .
    • UEFA Champions League = runner-up in ( 2019 - 2020 )
  • Struggled with injuries at times but remained highly skillful and marketable .

Al-Hilal ( 2023 - 2025 ) →

----------------------------------------------------

  • August 2023 : - Joined Al-Hilal in Saudi Arabia on a two-year contract .
  • Became one of the highest-paid players in football history .
  • Move was part of the Saudi Pro League's initiative to attract global stars .
  • He played their 6 matches where his total goal and assist contribution is 4 .
  • Neymar gets injured while he plays for Brazil ( Brazil vs Uruguay ) in Uruguay .
  • So , he suffered ACL injury for one more times .
  • After recovery he left the team Al-Hilal and joins Santos FC again for make him strong .

Santos FC ( 2025 - Present ) →

------------------------------------------------------------

  • After playing Al-Hilal for 1 year , he leaves Al-Hilal and joins his childhood club .
  • At Santos he trained himself very strong for prepare him for WORLDCUP 2026 .
  • Altough , he joins Santos FC to prove himself again in the world .

International Career with Brazil →

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Debut for Brazil in 2010 at age 18 .
  • Highlights : -
    • 2011 Copa America = quarterfinals exit
    • 2013 FIFA Confederations Cup = Brazil won ; Neymar received the Golden Ball as best player .
    • 2014 FIFA World Cup = Scored 4 goals but suffered a back injury in the quarterfinals vs Colombia ; missed later matches — Brazil lost to Germany in the semifinal .
    • 2016 Rio Olympics = Won gold medal ; Neymar scored the winning penalty in the final vs Germany .
    • 2021 Copa America = Reached the final (lost to Argentina) .
    • 2022 FIFA World Cup = Scored twice ; Brazil knocked out by Croatia in the quarterfinals .

  • As of 2023 , became Brazil's all-time top scorer , surpassing Pelé , Neymar total goal for his Brazil national team is 79 .

Playing Style →

------------------------------------------

Known for his dribbling , flair , creativity , speed , agility , freestyle skills , clinical finishing and playmaking . Often compared to Pelé and Ronaldinho for his Brazilian style of football .

-------------------------------------------

Legacy

-------------------------------------------
